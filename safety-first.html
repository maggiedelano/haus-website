how to have a safe roast

haus & courtyard access

All members of the MIT community are welcome in the courtyard to see bands, mud wrestle, and partake in various other festivities.

If you're an alum, you should have received an invitation in the mail. Bring that invitation; it'll get you haus access.

You need to be registered as a resident's guest to gain entry to haus after 1am.

policy on substance use

Alcohol consumption is not permitted in the lobby and other common areas inside haus.

The Muddy will be serving beer and wine to 21+ attendees in the courtyard.

Drug use and overdrinking will not be tolerated. Student Security can and will ask you to leave.

We've worked hard this year to demonstrate our commitment to health and safety, and we expect you to respect our community and make safe choices.
